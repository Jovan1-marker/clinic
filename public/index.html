About the Clinic

The School Clinic is dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services to all students. Our team of trained medical professionals ensures that every student receives prompt and proper medical attention. From routine checkups to emergency first aid, we are here to support the health and wellness of our school community. The clinic operates during school hours and maintains detailed medical records for each student visit.

Clinic Services

🩺

General Consultation

Walk-in consultations for common illnesses, headaches, stomach aches, and general health concerns.

🩹

First Aid Treatment

Immediate treatment for minor injuries, cuts, bruises, and sports-related incidents.

📋

Health Assessment

Periodic health screenings including height, weight, BMI, and vital signs monitoring.

💊

Medicine Dispensing

Over-the-counter medications available for common ailments with proper documentation.

📄

Medical Certificates

Issuance of medical certificates for excused absences and health clearances.

🧠

Health Counseling

Guidance on nutrition, hygiene, mental health awareness, and healthy lifestyle habits.
